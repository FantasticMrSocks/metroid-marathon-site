Metroid Marathon is a weekend-long celebration of Metroid. Join us as we play through the whole Metroid series, and raise money for Extra Life! This year's marathon will take place from August 30th to September 2nd.

Metroid Marathon is supporting Extra Life, a charity that unites thousands of gamers around the world to play games in support of their local Children's Miracle Network Hospital. Since its inception in 2008, Extra Life has raised more than $40 million for sick and injured kids.

DEFINITION: Slench Pizza /slen(t)SH ˈpētsə/

A pizza whose toppings are defined by charitable donations.

On Sunday evening, the team will eat some pizza together! What toppings will be on it? You decide! Donate and mention a topping you'd like to vote against or in support of! When the time comes, if a topping has more dollars toward it than against, we'll add it to the pizza!

Hate pineapple? Vote against it! Have a craving for anchovies? Vote for it! Dream of forcing us to eat a no-cheese-left-beef pizza? Don't let your dreams be dreams - donate and make it happen!

We'll be getting one pizza with all resultant toppings, and one pizza without any meat for our vegetarian team members, so don't worry about violating someone's dietary restrictions

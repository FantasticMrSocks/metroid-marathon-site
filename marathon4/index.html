Metroid Marathon is a weekend-long celebration of Metroid. Join us as we play through the whole Metroid series, and raise money for Extra Life! This year's marathon will take place from August 30th to September 2nd.

The marathon hasn't started yet, but the fun has! Join us for Guaranteed Hype right up to the start of the marathon.

Metroid Marathon is supporting Extra Life, a charity that unites thousands of gamers around the world to play games in support of their local Children's Miracle Network Hospital. Since its inception in 2008, Extra Life has raised more than $40 million for sick and injured kids.

About the Marathon

The Metroid Charity Marathon is a weekend-long celebration of the Metroid series. This year, we raised $178 for Extra Life!

About the Charity

Extra Life unites thousands of players around the world to support Children's Miracle Network Hospitals. Since its inception in 2008, Extra Life has raised more than $14 million for local CMN hospitals.
To make a donation, go to http://extra-life.org/participant/Ganon11.

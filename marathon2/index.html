Charity

Metroid Marathon is supporting Extra Life, a charity through which gamers raise money for Children's Miracle Network hospitals in their area. We are playing in support of Connecticut Children's Medical Center in Hartford, Connecticut. 100% of your donations go directly to the hospital.
